About Us

Ocean Energy is owned and operated by professional engineers in United Kingdom, providing efficient solutions to your engineering needs in no time.

We have extensive experience in Gas processing facilities, Cryogenic plants (LNG, LPG, NGL) Refineries, Petrochemical Plants, , Storage Facilities, and Utilities.

We are a group of Subject Matter Experts (SMEs) each having 15+ years experience in large and small hydrocarbon projects. We provide excellent services to our clients in Europe, Middle East and South Asia.

We perform Feasibility studies, Concept Selection, Front End Engineering Design, Detailed Engineering, Procurement Support, Technical Bid Evaluation, Commissioning, Startup and Performance Test. We conduct Hazop and QRA studies on routine basis.
